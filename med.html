Common Diseases

Fever Cold Cough
Body Pains Vomitings Motions

Fever

Symptoms: High body temperature, chills, headache, muscle aches.

Common medicines for fever include: Paracetamol, Ibuprofen, etc.

Cold

Symptoms: Runny or stuffy nose, sneezing, sore throat, cough.

Common medicines for cold include: Antihistamines, Decongestants, etc.

Cough

Symptoms: Persistent cough, chest discomfort, shortness of breath.

Common medicines for cough include: Cough syrups, Antitussives, etc.

Body Pains

Symptoms: Aching muscles, joint pain, discomfort in the body.

Common medicines for body pains include: Painkillers, Muscle relaxants, etc.

Vomitings

Symptoms: Nausea, throwing up, stomach discomfort.

Common medicines for vomitings include: Antiemetics, Bismuth subsalicylate, etc.

Motions

Symptoms: Diarrhea, loose stools, stomach cramps.

Common medicines for motions include: Oral rehydration solution (ORS), Antidiarrheal agents, etc.
